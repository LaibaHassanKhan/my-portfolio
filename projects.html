Project Gallery

Sky Shelf


This library project is a collaborative effort, with a dedicated page solely designed and developed by Laiba Hassan. The page offers a comprehensive digital library experience, allowing users to search for books easily. It includes sections for comics, magazines, novels, and a separate section for dictionaries. Additionally, the library features a “News Room” where users can read newspapers online. The design focuses on intuitive navigation and user-friendly access to all reading materials, making it an organized and engaging digital library.

Sugar Patel Pastries


This fast food project is a collaborative effort. The project provides a convenient digital fast food ordering experience, allowing users to easily browse and order their favorite meals right at their doorstep. It includes sections for burgers, pizzas, fries, sandwiches, and a dedicated section for beverages. Additionally, the system features a “Deals & Combos” section where users can explore special offers and meal bundles. The design emphasizes smooth navigation and a user-friendly interface, making it simple and engaging for customers to enjoy quick and hassle-free food ordering online. The project is built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and incorporates a touch of Bootstrap for responsiveness.

Sasta Bazar


Sasta Bazar is an individual project solely designed and developed by Laiba Hassan. The project provides a convenient online grocery shopping experience, allowing users to easily browse and order essential grocery items at their doorstep. It includes sections for rice, lentils, spices, oils, and other daily household essentials. Additionally, Sasta Bazar features a “Special Offers” section where users can explore discounts and seasonal deals. The design emphasizes smooth navigation and a user-friendly interface, making it simple and reliable for customers to shop for their groceries online.
